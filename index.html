    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about Meru Doctors Plaza.

    What are the opening hours of Meru Doctors Plaza?

    Meru Doctors Plaza operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing round-the-clock medical care.

    Do I need an appointment to see a doctor?

    It is recommended to book an appointment to avoid waiting times, but we also accept walk-ins based on doctor availability.

    What services are available at Meru Doctors Plaza?

    We offer a range of services including general consultations, specialist care (pediatrics, cardiology, gynecology, etc.), laboratory services, diagnostic imaging, and pharmacy services.

    Does Meru Doctors Plaza accept insurance?

    Yes, we accept most major insurance providers, including NHIF, Jubilee, Britam, AAR, and many more. Please contact us to confirm if we accept your insurance plan.

    How do I book an appointment at Meru Doctors Plaza?

    You can book an appointment by calling our reception desk, using our online booking system on our website, or by visiting us in person at the Plaza.

    Where is Meru Doctors Plaza located?

    Meru Doctors Plaza is located in Meru town, along Njuri Ncheke Street, opposite Meru County Headquarters.

    What should I bring to my appointment?

    Please bring your ID, insurance card, and any medical records relevant to your condition. If you are a new patient, you may also need to complete a registration form upon arrival.
